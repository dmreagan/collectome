Collectome

A content-sharing platform to facilitate the building of a community of practice around tiled video walls or similar large-format displays.

An Immersive Scholar project.

Have a tiled video wall?

The Collectome can help you curate, find, and display content for your wall.

Create

Curate a collection of media that fits your display.

Share

View community collections and remix them to suit your needs. Share your own collections.

Display

Point a fullscreen web browser at your collection or playlist for an immersive exhibit.
